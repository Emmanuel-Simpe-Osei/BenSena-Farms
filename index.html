Our Farm in Hohoe, Ghana

BenSena Farms Estate is a rice farming business located in Hohoe, Ghana, addressing the nation's dependency on rice imports by providing high-quality, locally grown rice.

With a growing demand for rice in Ghana, BenSena Farms aims to sustainably scale production, create jobs, and contribute to food security.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

"To sustainably cultivate and provide high-quality agricultural products for the Ghanaian market, reducing reliance on imports, supporting local agriculture, and empowering our community through job creation and food security initiatives."

Vision Statement

"To become a leading provider of locally grown food in Ghana, driving economic growth, promoting food self-sufficiency, and contributing to a sustainable agricultural future for our country."
